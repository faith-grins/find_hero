Introduction                                                             <ITRD>

Welcome to my "Ultimate Agarest Hero" FAQ!  This FAQ is meant to be a thorough
collection of all relevant information to the heroes of Agarest in one spot.

This FAQ will cover the following:

*  The statistics of a hero, sorted by lineage.  That means his base stats,
the growth rates, the affects of Proof of Valiant.  

*  Weapon choices based on a hero's lineage, including the skill slots given
both by weapon choice and added by way of Proof of Valiant.

*  Willpowers gained by the hero.  From the second generation onward, there
is some wiggle room to change Willpower based on when you use the Proof of
Valiant.  While it is generally advised to use the Proof of Valiant as soon
as possible, there may be a lineage or two where the Willpower gained by not
using a Proof of Valiant is superior.

*  Extended areas for each hero.

This FAQ will not cover any of the following:

*  A walkthrough for the game.  There are plenty of sources available for
that, and this is not one of them.

*  A "how to" for playing Record of Agarest War.

*  Advice for choices regarding the heroines and how to increase their
affection for the hero.  If you use this guide to plan out which heroines
you wish your heroes to marry, it is easy enough through common sense to
woo one particular heroine and maximize her affection.  If you need a guide,
those are available elsewhere on the internet.

Frequently Asked Questions                                                <FAQ>

Q:  Which version of the game was used to write this walkthrough?

A:  I have the EU disc-based version, so that's what I used.  There are some
translation differences to be noted, which is why I've included a Willpower
glossary with the Willpower section.  Some have different names, but the
definitions should be eaqsy enough to follow.

Q:  My preferred heroine only "Likes" my hero, and doesn't "Love" him.  Does
it matter?

A:  A heroine at "Like" affection will give you a decidedly inferior hero for
the next generation as opposed to a heroine at "Love" affection.  The next
generation's hero's base attributes will be the same or lower (usually lower),
the growth rates for said attributes will suffer from the same decline, and a
Proof of Valiant will also have diminished effects.  Sometimes, a hero from
the union of a Hero and Heroine at "Like" status will wield different weapons
than one from a "Love" relationship.

Q:  What impact does a mother play?

A:  The mother is the strongest influence on the hero of the next generation.
Most often, a hero will resemble his mother in combat in some fashion -- a
mage heroine generally produces mage offspring, and warrior to warrior.  The
mother determines what a hero looks like, what Willpowers he learns, and what
his extended area will be.  The mother also contributes one of the extra skill
slots learned by a Hero when he uses a Proof of Valiant.

Q:  What impact does a grandmother, great grandmother, etc., play?

A:  The grandmother determines the other skill slot learned by a Hero when he
uses a Proof of Valiant.  Beyond that, the cumulative lineage of a hero will
determine a hero's base attributes and growth rates, as well as his weapon of
choice.  There isn't a hard and fast formula for this one, at least not one
that has been discovered.  Weapon choices often lean towards those wielded by
the hero's parents, grandparents, etc.

Q:  Why does my hero wield a sword in cut-scenes when he uses a rod (or
scythe, or gun, or whatever else)?

A:  I don't know.  Maybe the developers were lazy.  Maybe it didn't occur to
them.  Maybe they decided that a sword "looked cool".  Pick your own reason.

The Heroes                                                                <HRO>

If all you are looking for are the nuts and bolts on a particular hero, that 
follows below.  You can use <Ctrl - F>, the key for a given hero is his 
generation number, followed by the first letter of each heroine in his 
lineage.  For example, a 3rd generation hero whose lineage was Vira-Lorr and
Sharona would be <3VS>, while a 5th generation hero whose lineage was 
Winfield, Arbol, Ganz, and Fer would be <5WAGF>.

Willpower                                                                 <WPR>

Willpowers are determined solely by the mother of the hero in question.  Also,
any willpower gained after level 50 is subject to a binary modifier:  has the
hero used a Proof of Valiant or not.  Note that Leonhardt gains all four of
his willpower before level 50, so the use of a Proof of Valiant is irrelevant.

The number in parentheses indicates the level at which the willpower is gained.

The names given for Willpowers are those used in the European version.  A
glossary follows below to define the skills, for those that differ from their
North American counterparts.

Leonhardt:  Unleash All, Parry (24), Energy, Genius (32)

Ladius (Elaine):  Bullishness, Genius (20)

Ladius (Elaine, no POV):  Kung Fu Qigong (53), Boundless Power (55)

Ladius (Fyuria):  Bullishness, Parry (20)

Thoma (Sherufanir):  Genius, Parry

Willpower Glossary                                                       <WPGL>

Absolute Hits -- Physical hits are 100% accurate when SP is 100 or more
Anger -- Increases physical damage dealt when SP is 200 or more
Boundless Power -- Increases physical damage and defense when HP is below 25%
Bullishness -- Increases the SP gained
Critical Break -- Incraeses magical damage dealt when SP is 200 or more
Energy -- Increases the AP gain in the character's extended area
Float -- Nullifies some skills, burst, and directional effects
Genius -- Reduces the AP cost of skills when SP is 150 or more
Guard -- Increases defense when SP is 150 or more
Kill Switch -- Increases accuracy and critical rate when SP is 100 or more
Kung Fu Qigong -- Voids damage equal to level * 3 when SP is 100 or more
Mikiri -- Blocks all counter-attacks
Parry -- Near-perfect evasion of physical attacks when HP is below 25%
Potential -- Bonus points increased at level up
Satori -- Increases evasion and accuracy when SP is 100 or more
Total Resistance -- Increases all of the character's elemental resistances
Unleash All -- All stats up when HP is below 25%
Unleash Magic -- Increases magic and magic defense when HP is below 25%
